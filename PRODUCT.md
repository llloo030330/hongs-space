# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Visitors reviewing Hong's personal work: product experiments, interactive web projects, photography, and small independent tools. They arrive from a public portfolio link and need a calm, credible overview that works on desktop and mobile.

## Product Purpose
Hong's Space is a public personal website for presenting finished experiments, selected projects, photography series, and contact information. Success means the site feels stable, personal, restrained, and ready to share.

## Brand Personality
Quiet, precise, personal. The site should feel like a maintained digital archive rather than a startup landing page, SaaS template, or game portal.

## Anti-references
Avoid exaggerated marketing copy, fake metrics, generic portfolio templates, loud cards, neon effects, heavy shadows, medical claims for Brain Garden, and redesigns that overpower the glass-cube Hero.

## Design Principles
1. Keep real work and real links visible without over-selling them.
2. Preserve the Hero, Gallery, and Brain Garden systems unless a verified bug requires a minimal fix.
3. Prefer subtle structure, clear spacing, and copy that sounds human.
4. Make mobile layouts complete and usable without hiding overflow problems.
5. Keep photography and experiments distinct, but part of the same quiet archive.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Target WCAG AA fundamentals: semantic headings, visible focus states, keyboard-operable lightbox controls, useful alt text, reduced-motion support, and touch targets that are comfortable on mobile.
